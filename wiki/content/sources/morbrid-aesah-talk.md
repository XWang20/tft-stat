## Key Takeaways
- **Advanced Mode**: 95%/99% CI worst case sorting — penalizes low sample, not high sample
- **Frequency vs AVP scatter plot (Graph View)**: draw diagonal line, above=OP, below=underperforming
  - morbrid: "I would love if somebody did a university project to really break down survivorship bias"
  - Tier list algorithm = mix of frequency + place change (quantifies the diagonal)
- **Exclude → Toggle On method**: exclude all anti-heal → see AVP jump → measures category importance
  - Anti-heal and shred roughly equally important in Vanguard Marksman
- **Games tab sanity check**: always verify filter quality by looking at real games
  - Click suspicious outlier → add to filter → look at games → exclude if garbage
- **Bookmarks**: save two filter sets, toggle between for A/B comparison
- **Plus-one traits**: MetaTFT detects extra emblems from golems/training dummies
- **Don't specify star level** in filters — introduces selection bias (morbrid)
- **Item economy matters**: Shojin on Zoe bad in Rebel (means Jinx doesn't have it) but good in Sorc
- Patch early → trust individuals. Patch late → trust data. (morbrid + Aesah)
