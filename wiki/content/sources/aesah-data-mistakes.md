## Key Takeaways
- **Play rate weighted formula**: the core contribution
  ```
  no_item_AVP = (overall - p × item_AVP) / (1 - p)
  necessity = no_item_AVP - overall_AVP
  ```
- Heimer example: Guard Breaker AVP (4.01) looks way better than Archangels (4.21), but play rate weighting shows Archangels has 3.5× more real impact
- Low play rate items appear strong because of late-game carousel acquisition
- Play rate + AVP inversely correlated = systematic, not random
- Base stats on items matter (not just the passive effect)
- Giant Slayer uses sword+bow (bad AP components) vs Rabadon uses rod+rod (good) — item economy matters beyond pure DPS
