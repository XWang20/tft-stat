## Key Takeaways
- **"Add context first"**: Don't search "Zoe items" — search "7 Rebel Zoe items" or "6 Sorc Zoe items"
  - Same Zoe: Rageblade > Shojin in Rebel, Shojin > Rageblade in Sorc
- **Look at builds, not single items**: "item combos and full builds knock out a lot of the bias"
  - Two-item combos often sufficient
- **Sample size is the most underrated factor**: "I've talked to a lot of pro players and almost nobody understands sample size"
  - Mature patch: ≥1000 games; new patch: ≥300 games
- **Stage 5/6 carousel bias**: "that's just a very constant bias for every single item in every set"
- **Play rate = confidence signal**: high play rate + good delta = trust it; low play rate + great delta = investigate
- **Dirty secret: filter by GM+ and specific regions** — see what KR vs NA does differently
- **Augment stats workaround**: search for item proxies (e.g., 4× Rageblade = Anger Issues)
- **Units tab for capping boards**: add your 7 units, go to Units tab, look at frequency + delta for last slots
- **Traits tab for breakpoints**: search trait names, compare 4 vs 6 vs 8 delta to decide breakpoints
